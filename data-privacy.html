Data Deletion Policy

Effective Date: February 19, 2025

1. Introduction

At CNHS Alumni Portal, we respect your right to control your personal data. This policy explains how users can request data deletion.

2. How to Request Data Deletion

  • Send an email to nikkersondoydora02@gmail.com with the subject "Data Deletion Request."
  • Provide your registered email address and relevant account details.
  • Your request will be processed within 7-14 business days.

3. What Data Will Be Deleted

  • Profile information (Name, Email, Contact Details)
  • Graduation details (Batch, Year, Course)
  • Any other personal data associated with your account

4. Exceptions

Some data may be retained for legal, security, or administrative purposes. If this applies to your request, we will notify you accordingly.

5. Contact Us

For further inquiries about data deletion, please reach out to nikkersondoydora02@gmail.com.
